About Afiya Medium Clinic

A community‑focused clinic providing affordable, quality care for Gewane Woreda and surrounding areas in Afar Region, Ethiopia. We offer patient‑centered services with respect for local culture and languages (Amharic • Afar), ensuring timely access to outpatient, maternal & child health, pharmacy, and referral services.

  • Accessible, compassionate care delivered by qualified professionals
  • Maternal & Child Health focus: antenatal, postnatal, immunization, nutrition, family planning
  • Licensed pharmacy & basic laboratory for rapid support
  • Active health education & outreach for the community
  • Trusted referral pathways to higher‑level facilities when needed
  • Open daily with clear communication in Amharic and Afar
Founder: Mohammed Mersha Assefa
